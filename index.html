BCM Insurance Limited

B.C Madiebo & Company Insurance Brokers Limited.

BCM is a duly registered insurance brokerage firm established since 1963. Our business has been built on trust and sustained by reputation. We specialize in all classes of insurance in order to ensure that you are insured. With almost 60 years of being in business comes ample experience and with it comes knowledge.

Whilst we have grown to understand the science of insurance, we have also learnt that people and not science are the reason that our business has continued to thrive.

This is why our customers are very important to us. Our customers are the core of our business and providing them with excellent services that meet their insurance needs and exceed their highest expectations is the basis of our business and the reason we remain first choice in minds of our customers.
