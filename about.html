What We Do?

Our primary functions as Insurance Brokers include among other things the following;

Peace of Mind

We act as an intermediary between insurers and customers. We do so by offering, negotiating and purchasing policies on behalf of our clients.
We also provide advice in the interest of our clients and help them outline risk management strategies that are best suited to their profile; giving our customers the peace of mind they deserve when it comes to protecting their assets.

Cost Reduction

Working with us and taking advantage of our knowledge and experience in the Insurance industry enables our customers avoid unnecessary costs.
We are uniquely equipped to save you money on your insurance because our pricing is often more cost effective than direct insurance pricing.
Insurance companies provide us with lower rates because we often buy on behalf of a group of individuals/businesses and because we are professionally trained to accurately assess risk and as such help customers choose policies that adequately suit them.
Purchasing insurance through BCM will allow us evaluate your risk, lower your risk, lower your premium and as such reduce your liabilities.

Claim Settlement

We report, follow up and ensure that our customers claims are settled in a timely fashion by their underwriter leaving them with ample time to focus on other important aspects of their business.

Risk Management

In the area of risk management, we advise our clients on the risks that are uneconomically insurable or otherwise, e.g. In Burglary insurance we have had cases where our clients have arranged to include bulky items in their insurance policy and we have had to advise against it as these items cannot be easily lifted or stolen.
We go deep into our clients exposures, identify the risks and categorize them to the benefit of our client.

Appropriate Cover

We ensure that our clients have the most appropriate insurance protection by assessing their risks and giving them only those policies that are best suited to their needs.
We also ensure that in placing their risks, we do so with with the most appropriate underwriters.
